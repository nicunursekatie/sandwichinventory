Host a Sandwich-Making Event

Turn a few hours with your group into hundreds of meals for your neighbors.
Every week, kids, seniors, and families across Metro Atlanta rely on sandwiches from The Sandwich Project. When your group hosts a sandwich-making event, you're putting real food directly into the hands of people who need it — no middleman, no complicated logistics. Just your crew, some supplies, and a couple of hours.
📋 What It Looks Like
  • 1.5–2 hours from start to cleanup
  • 5–30+ volunteers — scales to any group size
  • A clean table-height surface (kitchen, fellowship hall, conference room — anywhere works)
  • Access to a fridge for deli events
  • Great for churches, offices, scout troops, schools, families, friend groups
🥪 What You'll Make
  • PB&J sandwiches — shelf-stable, easy, adult-assembled only (see note below)
  • Deli sandwiches (turkey or ham) — kid-friendly to assemble; requires fridge access & coolers
  • You can do one or both!
  • A group of 10 can make 100+ sandwiches in under 2 hours
  • We'll send you exact recipes and portions — no guesswork
🤝 What We Handle vs. What You Handle
TSP Provides
  • Shopping list with exact quantities for your group size
  • Printable labels for every loaf
  • Step-by-step event day guide
  • Food safety guidance (simple — we promise)
  • Delivery coordination & drop-off location
  • Support before, during, and after your event
Your Group Handles
  • Purchasing ingredients (we give you the exact list)
  • Providing the space and table surfaces
  • Rounding up volunteers
  • Assembling & packaging sandwiches
  • Delivering to the assigned drop-off point
⚠️ Youth Policy: Volunteers under 18 are not permitted to assemble PB&J sandwiches due to allergen safety protocols. Exceptions require express written permission from the TSP Safety Lead. Deli sandwich assembly is open to all ages.
