Hunger Hits Home

36% of Georgia families with children face food insecurity—more than double the national average
1 in 6 children in Metro Atlanta go to bed hungry
57% of food-insecure Georgians earn too much for SNAP
Most food-insecure families have jobs—they're choosing between rent and groceries, gas and dinner
Source: The State of Child Health and Well-Being in Georgia 2025, Emory University Rollins School of Public Health
