    💡 AI Planning Tips

    Station Setup

    • Set up assembly line: bread → protein → cheese/condiment → close → bag
    • One person per station for efficiency
    • Use gloves and change between tasks

    Deli Assembly

    • 2 slices bread, meat (2-4 oz), 2 slices cheese per sandwich
    • Fold meat for even coverage
    • Place in sandwich bag immediately

    PB&J Assembly

    • Spread ~3 tbsp PB on one slice
    • Spread ~1.5 tbsp jelly on the other
    • Press together gently, bag immediately
    • One spatula per ~100 sandwiches

    Temperature Control

    • Keep deli meat & cheese below 40°F until assembly
    • Assembled sandwiches safe at room temp for up to 2 hours
    • Use coolers with ice packs for transport

    Hygiene

    • Wash hands before and during prep
    • Wear disposable gloves
    • Clean all surfaces before starting
    • Check all expiration dates

    Timeline

    • 1 week before: Purchase non-perishables, count supplies
    • 1-2 days before: Buy bread, meat, cheese
    • Day of: Set up stations, assemble, pack, distribute

    Tips

    • Buy 5-10% extra to account for waste
    • Label bags with contents and date
    • Keep a count as you go
    • Plan for ~30-40 sandwiches per volunteer per hour
