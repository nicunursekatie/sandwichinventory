🧮 Sandwich Calculator

What is this?

This calculator helps you plan a sandwich-making event for The Sandwich Project. It figures out exactly how much food to buy and what it will cost — so you don't overbuy or run short.

How to use it (3 simple steps):

Step 1: Pick your sandwich type below — Deli Meat or PB&J.

Step 2: Tell us either how many sandwiches you want to make, or how much money you have to spend.

Step 3: Choose your brands and options, then hit "Calculate" — we'll show you a full shopping list with quantities and costs.

Using a different brand? Select "Enter custom package" at the bottom of any ingredient dropdown to enter your own weight, slice count, and price.

⚠️ Reminders: Use pre-packaged deli meat only (not sliced at the deli counter). For cheese, use deli-style sliced American cheese — not Kraft singles or any individually plastic-wrapped slices.

🥪 Assembly Instructions

🥪 Deli Sandwich Assembly

2slices bread (no heels)
2slices cheese (one per bread slice)
2-3slices deli meat (follow package serving size)
1zip-lock sandwich bag
Meat tip: Check the package label—if serving size = 2 slices (thick cut), use 2. If serving size = 3 slices (thin cut), use 3. Goal is ~2 oz per sandwich.
Assembly Order: Bread → Cheese → Meat → Cheese → Bread

🛒 Shop Smart

✅ Shopping Standards

  • Pre-packaged deli meat only (not sliced at deli counter)
  • Same quality you'd serve family
  • 10+ days until expiration
  • Add 5-10% buffer for extras
✅ Right Cheese Deli-style sliced American cheese (NOT individually wrapped)
❌ Wrong Cheese Kraft singles or any individually plastic-wrapped slices — they're a pain to unwrap, they're "cheese product" (not real cheese), and they turn into a gross mess on sandwiches

🛡️ REQUIRED: Food Safety Guidelines

Every volunteer must follow these rules — no exceptions.

Our recipients include children, elderly, and immunocompromised individuals. These rules protect them.

🧤
Before You Start
  • Wash hands with soap & warm water
  • Gloves on — required at all times
  • Hair & beards tied back or netted
  • Clean & cover all prep surfaces
  • Pre-packaged meat only (no deli counter)
  • Check dates: expires 10+ days out
🥪
While Making Sandwiches
  • Keep ingredients at 40°F or below
  • ⚠️ Never leave ingredients at 80°F+
  • One meat package out of fridge at a time
  • Change gloves after touching non-food items
  • Return finished loaves to fridge immediately
  • Keep nut products away from deli area
  • When in doubt, throw it out!
📦
Packing Up
  • Refrigerate 30 min before packing into coolers
  • ⚠️ Never put warm sandwiches in coolers
  • Coolers must be cool first (not hot from garage)
  • Ice packs touching every loaf
  • Pack tight — fill completely, no air gaps
  • ~100 sandwiches per large cooler
🚚
Transport & Delivery
  • Don't reopen — coolers heat up fast
  • Go direct — straight to host locations
  • Family quality: feed others what you'd feed yours
⚠️ REMEMBER: Our recipients include children, elderly, and immunocompromised individuals. Every rule above exists to protect them.
