Looking for a Service Activity?

Make a Difference with The Sandwich Project

Looking for a service activity for your family, team, scout troop, or book club? Consider making sandwiches and collecting healthy snacks and fruit for The Sandwich Project (TSP).

TSP is a 501(c)(3) non-profit organization whose volunteers assemble and donate fresh, homemade sandwiches each week for immediate consumption to meet the food scarcity needs in Metro Atlanta. They also collect fresh fruit (apples, green bananas, oranges) and protein bars.

What We Need:

  • Fresh, homemade sandwiches
  • Fresh fruit (apples, green bananas, oranges)
  • Healthy protein bars

Making an Impact

1.4M+ Sandwiches Distributed
70+ Charities Served

Since the organization began, they have distributed more than 1.4 million sandwiches and serve 70+ charities across Metro Atlanta.
