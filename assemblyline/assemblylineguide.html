Group Assembly Guide

The Sandwich Project - For Event Organizers
How It Works: The entire group works on ONE stage at a time. Fill your workspace with bread, then everyone moves together to the next step.

Assembly Stages

1

Lay Out Bread

  • Everyone spreads out around table/counter
  • Lay bread slices side by side until workspace is filled
  • No ends or heels
  • Count slices to track sandwich count
2

Add Cheese

  • Everyone adds one slice of cheese to EACH piece of bread
  • Work systematically so no bread gets missed
  • Call out if you see any bread without cheese
3

Add Meat

  • Everyone adds meat based on your portion plan:

2 Slices Total

Put 1 slice on EACH piece of bread. When closed = 2 slices per sandwich.

3 Slices Total

Put 1 slice on half, 2 slices on other half. Match carefully when closing.

4

Close & Bag

  • Match up halves and close sandwiches
  • Place each sandwich in individual zip-lock bag
  • Press out excess air, seal tightly
5

Pack Loaves & Refrigerate

  • Place bagged sandwiches into loaf bags
  • Secure with twist tie (no knots or rubber bands)
  • IMMEDIATELY to refrigerator - no delays!
